ABOUT OUR APP

Key features of our app

Advise

Students have access to videos and articles from others who have experienced the issues. They no longer have to worry about dealing with college preparation, sports, or grades alone because they can recieve guidance and advice from peers that are most likely going through the same thing as them.

Connect

SiNC directly connects students with their classes and clubs so they can stay up-to-date and and connect with their peers. SiNC also allows students to merge into new friend groups.

Mobile

This app provides on the go access to students of the 21st Century, since phones are a necessity for all students.

Support

SiNC's main goal is to provide a resource where high school students can come together and help each other strive in school amd make new friendships with people who have the same interests as them.

Mission Statement

A lot of the kids don't know how to approach new people or if they will have anything in common. A way for them to have an idea of who they can talk to before they even get there, and that's where we come in. Our app is just an option for students who have a hard time with approaching new people in person, I'm sure we all know how it can be as a freshman and being in a new place with a lot more people. We want to get rid of that awkward stage where they get to high school.

Our Vision

We may be new to the game, but we do know how to make something look good. Making a user-friendly interface is the most important thing to us. Ensuring all people from all kinds of technological backgrounds can operate SiNC.
